\begin{abstract}
We formalize the Merged Quantum Gauge and Scalar Consciousness Framework (MQGT--SCF), which supplements General Relativity and the Standard Model with two real scalar fields: a consciousness field $\Phic(x)$ and an ethical field $\Eth(x)$. We present a compact renormalizable Lagrangian density, derive the Euler--Lagrange equations, specify quantization and interaction structure for the new sectors, and provide testable predictions (e.g., ethically weighted Born-rule deviations, interferometric phase shifts). The framework admits a holographic embedding and suggests experimental programs involving quantum random number generators, interferometry, gravitational waves, and neural recordings.
\end{abstract}

\section{Introduction}
A minimal extension of the Standard Model (SM) and General Relativity (GR) that includes mind and value as dynamical fields can be written in local, Lorentz-invariant form. In MQGT--SCF the pair $(\Phic, \Eth)$ encodes subjective awareness and moral valence, respectively, allowing measurement, agency, and teleological bias to be represented inside quantum field theory. This paper gives the Lagrangian, field equations, quantization, and primary empirical consequences.

\section{Unified Lagrangian}
Let $g_{\mu\nu}$ be the spacetime metric with determinant $g$, and $\psi$ denote SM fermions. The total Lagrangian density is
\begin{equation}
\label{eq:Ltot}
\Ltot = \LGR + \LSM + \frac{1}{2}\partial_\mu \Phic\,\partial^\mu \Phic - V(\Phic) + \frac{1}{2}\partial_\mu \Eth\,\partial^\mu \Eth - U(\Eth)
+ \xi\,\Phic\,\Eth + \lambda_c\,\Phic\,\bar\psi\psi + \lambda_e\,\Eth\,\bar\psi\gamma^5\psi + \LZora(\Phic,\Eth;\Psi),
\end{equation}
with quartic potentials
\begin{equation}
V(\Phic)=\frac{\mu_c^2}{2}\,\Phic^2+\frac{\kappa_c}{4}\,\Phic^4,\qquad
U(\Eth)=\frac{\mu_e^2}{2}\,\Eth^2+\frac{\kappa_e}{4}\,\Eth^4.
\end{equation}
Here $\LGR=\frac{\Mpl^2}{2}\,R$ is the Einstein--Hilbert term and $\LSM$ is the Standard Model Lagrangian in curved spacetime. The interaction couplings $(\xi,\lambda_c,\lambda_e)$ encode mind--matter, ethics--matter, and mind--ethics links; $\LZora$ collects higher-order, categorical/topological, or adaptive terms.

\subsection{Euler--Lagrange equations}
Varying~\eqref{eq:Ltot} gives
\begin{align}
\Box \Phic + V'(\Phic) &= \xi\,\Eth + \lambda_c\,\bar\psi\psi + \frac{\delta \LZora}{\delta \Phic}, \label{eq:eomPhi}\\
\Box \Eth + U'(\Eth) &= \xi\,\Phic + \lambda_e\,\bar\psi\gamma^5\psi + \frac{\delta \LZora}{\delta \Eth}, \label{eq:eomE}
\end{align}
and the Einstein equations $G_{\mu\nu}=M_{\mathrm{Pl}}^{-2}\,T_{\mu\nu}$ with stress tensor $T_{\mu\nu}$ including the new sectors. Primes denote derivatives with respect to the field argument and $\Box=\nabla^\mu\nabla_\mu$.
